Facilitator script · hands-on activity

Build your brand in 40 minutes

⏱ 40 min👥 novice-friendly🛠 one free AI tab🎁 walk out with a Brand Kit + a post

“Write down who you are once — then AI makes everything look like you.”

⚙️ Before you start, tell the room: open one tab of ChatGPT or Gemini (free) and keep it open the whole time. That's all anyone needs.
1
0:00 – 2:00 · Frame it

Set the scene

🎤 say this

“In the next 40 minutes you'll build your own brand — and make something real with it. No design skills needed.”

“The big idea is simple: AI gives everyone the same generic stuff unless you tell it who you are. So we'll write that down once — and then everything you make comes out looking like you.”

“By the end you'll have a reusable Brand Kit and one finished post. Don't overthink anything — there are no wrong answers today.”

2
2:00 – 6:00 · References

Find your taste

🎤 say this

“Open Pinterest and search ‘brand identity’. In 3 minutes, save 3 to 5 brands whose feeling you love — colours, mood, vibe.”

“You're not copying — you're showing the AI your taste, so it stops being generic.”

they doSave 3–5 reference images. Keep it quick — 3 minutes, then move on.
3
6:00 – 16:00 · Strategy

Let AI interview you

🎤 say this

“The trick: don't ask AI to make a brand for you — make it ask you questions first. Paste this, then answer one question at a time, honestly and briefly.”

📋 put on screen
You're a friendly brand strategist. Interview me ONE question
at a time about what I do, who I serve, and how I want to feel.
After 5 questions, give me: a one-line description, my brand
personality in 3 words, a short mission, and 5 tagline options.
they doAnswer in a sentence each. Then copy the 3 personality words + favourite tagline — we'll reuse them.
4
16:00 – 22:00 · Look

Colours + fonts

🎤 say this

“Same chat. Let's give your brand a look. Paste this — swap in your 3 personality words.”

📋 put on screen
For a brand that feels [your 3 words], give me:
1) a 5-colour palette with hex codes and where to use each, and
2) a heading + body font pairing that's free on Google Fonts.
Avoid the generic purple-to-blue gradient.
they doCopy the 5 hex codes and the 2 font names. Colours & type — done.
5
22:00 – 25:00 · Voice

“Last piece — your voice. No AI needed, just fill this in for yourself.”

6
25:00 – 30:00 · Keep it

Make your Brand Kit

🎤 say this

“Now bottle it all into one thing you'll reuse forever. Fill in this card. This is your Brand Block — paste it on top of any AI chat and it instantly knows who you are.”

BRAND:     —    
FOR:    
PERSONALITY:     (3 words)
COLOURS:     (your 5 hex)
FONTS:    /   
VOICE:    · avoid   
say“Keep this. This is the whole point of today.”
7
30:00 – 37:00 · Make it

Make one real thing

🎤 say this

“Let's prove it works — one Instagram post, on-brand. Open Gemini (it makes images free), paste your Brand Block, then this:”

📋 put on screen
[paste your Brand Block here]

Now make an Instagram post for me: headline "[your message]".
Use my colours and fonts, lots of white space, in my voice.
say“Not quite right? Don't say ‘make it better’ — be specific: ‘make the background #yourcolour’ or ‘bigger headline’. (Prefer no code? Do this in Canva with a template instead.)”
8
37:00 – 40:00 · Wrap

Save it + share

🎤 say this

“Save your Brand Block somewhere safe — or paste it into ChatGPT's Custom Instructions so it's always on.”

“That's it — you have a brand, and a post that matches it. Tomorrow: a poster, a deck, even a website — same Brand Block, every time. Who'll show us their post?”
