AI & Design

A hands-on session on using AI for design — and building your own brand you can reuse forever. Free tools first; you'll leave with something real.

Session 1 · 9:30 AM – 12:00 PM Session 2 · 1:00 – 3:30 PM Facilitators: Suhavi & Aditya Free-tools-first In person · bring a laptop
You are the Creative Director. AI is your genius intern.

Brilliant, fast, well-read — but junior. It does its best work when you brief it well, hand it references, and give feedback instead of expecting one-shot perfection. Everything we'll build today will come back to this one idea.

The AI tools we'll use

Most of these have a real free tier — plenty to learn and draft with. Explore widely, then keep the few that fit how you work. = works well on a phone.

The jobWhat we'll useWhat it's for in the session
Write & ideateChatGPT · Gemini · Claude Brainstorming, names, brand strategy & voice, and rewriting prompts into strong briefs.
Make imagesGemini ("Nano Banana") · ChatGPT · Leonardo Generating visuals, mood, and logo concepts. (Free image outputs can be public — don't use sensitive material.)
Assemble & finishCanva Where you actually build it — templates, social posts, and fixing AI's approximate colours to exact hex.
Vibe-code a websiteLovable · BoltDescribe the site in plain words; the AI builds the pages, layout and copy for you to tweak and publish.
Others…
Slide decksGammaFast first-draft decks — limited lifetime credits and a watermark.
AI videoRunway · Google VeoShort clips from a prompt — trial / limited free credits, not ongoing.
Two things to watch with "free": ① it can mean a daily reset (sustainable) or one-time lifetime credits (a trial — e.g. Gamma, Runway, Veo). ② free exports often carry watermarks or a vendor subdomain. Free is perfect to learn & draft; pay one tier (~$15–20/mo) only when it goes client-facing. Limits drift — we'll confirm exact numbers live.
