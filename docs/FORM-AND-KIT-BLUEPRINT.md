# Pre-Workshop Form + Takeaway Kit — blueprint (review before build)

---

## A) Pre-Workshop Google Form

**Purpose:** sent ~5 days before. Tells us skill level, device, goals, and brand seed — so we tailor the room and so Step 1 of the build starts warm, not cold. ~2 minutes to complete.
**What I'll deliver:** exact question copy + types, ready to paste into Google Forms, plus a one-screen setup guide. (I can't create the live Form for you, but you'll have copy-paste-ready text and structure.)

**Questions:**
1. Name + what you do (short text) — *art / public health / other.*
2. "I'd describe my AI experience as…" (single choice): Never used it · Tried it once or twice · Use it sometimes · Use it weekly+.
3. "Which of these have you used?" (checkboxes): ChatGPT · Gemini · Claude · Canva · Midjourney/Leonardo · None.
4. "What will you bring on the day?" (single choice): Laptop · Laptop + phone · Phone only. *(Drives which phone-friendly tools we lean on.)*
5. "By the end, the one thing I'd love to walk out with is…" (paragraph).
6. **Brand seed:** "In one sentence, who are you / what's your thing?" (paragraph) — *warms up Step 1.*
7. "Pick a vibe that's most 'you'." (single choice w/ words): Calm & trustworthy · Bold & energetic · Warm & human · Sharp & modern · Playful & creative.
8. Anything you're nervous or curious about with AI? (optional paragraph) — *surfaces fears/ethics to address.*
9. Consent: OK to show your screen/work in the gallery walk? (yes/no).

**Setup guide included:** how to turn on response summary, and a note to skim answers the night before to pick 2–3 live examples that match the room.

## B) "Brand-in-a-Box" Takeaway Kit

The thing they keep. Designed to work **with or without a paid plan** — the core is a one-page brief + copy-paste prompts that run on *any* AI tool. Delivered as clean, printable HTML (and I'll note how to save as PDF). Contents:

**1. The Brand Brief (one page, fill-in-the-blanks)** — the heart of the kit.
- Who I serve · What I stand for · Personality/archetype · Mission (1 line) · Tagline.
- Visual identity: 3–5 HEX colors · heading + body font · imagery style.
- Voice: 3 traits · words we use · words we never use.

**2. The Reusable "Brand Block."** A copy-paste header they drop at the top of any prompt so the intern always knows who they are. Plus the free vs. paid ways to store it (saved note ↔ Custom Instructions / Projects / custom GPT).

**3. Prompt Cheat-Sheet (the briefs).**
- Text-prompt formula card (Role·Task·Context·Constraints·Format·Examples) with a filled example.
- Image-prompt formula card (Subject·Medium·Composition·Lighting·Color·Mood·Style·Aspect) with a filled example.
- 6 ready-to-use "recipe" prompts: brand interview · color palette · logo concept · social post · brochure · "rewrite in my brand voice."

**4. The "Un-Dumb Your AI" card.** The fix kit from Segment 3 on one card: fresh chat · summarize-restart · be specific · give examples · steps · say what-not-to-do · ask it to argue against you · put key info first/last.

**5. The Free-Tool Map.** The verified job→free-pick→paid-upgrade table, with phone-friendly tags and the two gotchas (lifetime vs. daily credits; watermarks).

**6. The Responsible-Use card.** Copyright (own it = add human work), don't-copy-living-artists, disclose AI, counter-steer bias, never paste PII/patient data, verify every fact. Sourced + dated so it doesn't read as hand-waving.

**7. "Keep going" links.** The tools used + one good free guide each.
