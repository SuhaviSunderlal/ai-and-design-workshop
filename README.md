# AI & Design — Workshop

A 2.5-hour, hands-on workshop on using AI for design — and building your own brand you can reuse forever. Free-tools-first, written for an intelligent, non-technical audience (art + public health). By **Suhav & Aditya**, built in the EkhoPost design language.

## Running it

- **Present:** open `deck/index.html` in any browser (or use the live link).
  - `←` / `→` / `Space` — navigate · `N` — presenter notes · `F` — fullscreen
  - A 40-minute countdown timer appears during the build section (`T` start/pause).
- **Takeaway kit:** open `takeaway-kit/brand-in-a-box.html`, then print → Save as PDF.

## The arc (5 parts)

1. **Meet your intern** — how AI actually thinks (just enough to direct it).
2. **Keep it sharp** — why AI drifts mid-project, and the fixes.
3. **Make anything** — images, social, print, websites, digital products, decks. One recipe: a **reference + your design system** (colours, type, components) → AI generates it as **pixels** or **code**.
4. **Build your brand** — strategy → identity → voice → your reusable "system prompt."
5. **Take it home** — responsible use + the kit.
