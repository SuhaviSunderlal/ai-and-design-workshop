# Night-Before AI Pulse — paste-ready copy

A 45-second check to calibrate the room. Send tonight. Skim answers before you sleep.
*(This is the short, last-minute version. The fuller 9-question survey lives in `FORM-paste-ready.md` — use that one only when you have ~5 days' lead.)*

**Form title:** Quick hello before tomorrow 👋
**Form description:**
> 45 seconds, 4 questions — no wrong answers. This just helps us pitch tomorrow at exactly the right level for the room. See you there! — Suhav & Aditya

---

### Q1 — Multiple choice (required)
**How would you describe your experience with AI tools?**
- Never really used them
- Tried it once or twice
- I use it sometimes
- I use it most weeks / daily

### Q2 — Checkboxes (optional)
**Which of these have you actually used? (tick any)**
- ChatGPT
- Google Gemini
- Claude
- Canva (AI features)
- Midjourney / Leonardo / other image tools
- None yet

### Q3 — Multiple choice (required)
**How often do you reach for AI right now?**
- Never
- Once in a while
- A few times a month
- A few times a week
- Every day

### Q4 — Short answer / Paragraph (optional)
**One thing you're hoping to get out of tomorrow?**
*Help text:* A rough phrase is perfect — even "not sure yet, just curious" helps.

---

## Setup guide (60 seconds)
1. **forms.google.com → Blank form.** Paste the title + description.
2. Add each question with the **type in bold**. Toggle **Required** on Q1 + Q3.
3. **Settings:** "Make this a quiz" stays OFF. Under **Responses**, turn on **email notifications** so answers ping you tonight.
4. **Send → copy the short link →** drop it in the WhatsApp/email thread with: *"Quick 45-sec form before tomorrow 🙏"*.
5. **Before bed:** open **Responses → Summary**. Note where the room sits on Q1 + Q3, and grab 2–3 quotes from Q4 to read aloud in the opening.

## Why these four
- **Q1 (experience) + Q3 (frequency)** are different axes — how *good* vs. how *often*. Together they tell you whether to run the beginner-friendly open or skip the basics.
- **Q2 (tools)** decides which tools you demo live — use the ones people already have, not new logins.
- **Q4** gives you real quotes to open with, so the room feels seen in the first 5 minutes.
