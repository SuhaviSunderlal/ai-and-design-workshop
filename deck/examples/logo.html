Brand Proposal guidelines

Tactile Metaphors for Dementia Care

Strategic Design
01 — Logo Concepts (Oil Pastel Rendering)

1. The Shared Sparkle

The larger grandparent star nested alongside the tiny, bright child star. A symbol that memory may shift, but the magic of connection remains identical.

2. The Leaf & Seed

An autumn leaf offering shelter to a bright seedling. A natural transition metaphor showing generations protective of one another through new seasons.

3. The Heartstring

A single, continuous crayon scribble loop that forms a heart in the center of the generation gap. Speaks to continuous communication and play.

02 — Visual & Typographic Directions
Direction 01

The Paper Garden

Earthy, grounding, and storytelling-focused. Uses natural tones and cut-out shapes to frame changing seasons and memory decay as natural, peaceful occurrences.

The Memory Leaf
Grandpa’s mind is like a beautiful tree. Sometimes the autumn wind gently blows the leaves away, but the strong roots hold onto the love.
Direction 02

Playful Connections

Focused purely on active interaction, games, and laughter. Uses bright contrast doodles and bouncy, uneven letterforms to keep children engaged and confident.

Let's Play!
Even if Grandpa forgot what we had for breakfast, he definitely remembers how to sing our favorite song. Let's make some loud music!
Direction 03

The Midnight Magic

Rich, reassuring, and comforting. Relies on bedtime-story high-contrast indigo and glowing golds to make children feel incredibly safe navigating confusing emotions.

A Light in the Dark
Sometimes, the lightbulbs in Grandma's memory house turn off for the night. But we have a secret star flashlight to help her find them.
