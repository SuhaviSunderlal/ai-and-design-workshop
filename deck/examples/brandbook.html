The Brand Story (Back of Book)
"Every great tree starts as a tiny seed, sheltered by the leaves above it. As seasons change, leaves turn beautiful colors and gently drift down, but the roots beneath remain strong. Our books help children understand the changing seasons of their grandparents' minds, teaching them how to stand close, offer shelter, and grow together."
04 — Art Direction & Parent Guides

The Metaphor in Practice: We use the natural cycle of the leaf and seed to help parents explain memory changes. Infographic steps utilize off-register drop shadows and imperfect crayon contours to keep the guidance feeling gentle and artistic, never clinical.

Verbal Application Matrix
Instead of... We say...
"He is suffering from progressive cognitive decline." "Grandpa is entering a quiet season of remembering."
"It is so tragic when Grandpa forgets things." "Grandpa’s mind is resting, but his heart remembers how much he loves you."

How to stand close:

Speak warmly. Grandpa loves the soft sound of your voice.
If his thoughts get tangled, just hold his hand. Touch is magic.
